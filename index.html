Welcome to the Minecraft Collor Pallet Maker

Here you can make your own minecraft block pallets for your builds. There is variety of blocks to chose from and many many more will be added in the future. Enjoy building!

How it works?

Dont know how to make pallets? Not a problem. Here is the guide how to use our website.

To make you own pallets navigate to the "Pallet maker" tab of the website and there you will be able to chose from a variety of blocks.

If you know what block you are looking for there are filters on the left side of the page to help you find your desiered blocks quicker (blocks can be filtered by color, chategory or by name).

Once you have found the block that you want to add to your pallet then just click on the "Add block" button and the windowd will open where you will see all your pallets.

If you dont have any pallet already you will be able to create your own pallet by clicking on the "" sign in the top right. From there you can give your pallet a name and press "create". Once created your pallet will be dispayed there. All pallets can contain a maximum of 6 blocks. And for now you can create up to 6 pallets.

To add the block to the pallet that you want just click anywhere on the pallet and the block will be added to the pallet. If you want to add other blocks just press "Done" and find another block that you want to add to the pallet and repeat the proces until you made you own pallet the way you want.

By pressing "Clear pallets" button, all pallets that you have created can be deleted and you can make different ones. We hope that this guide was helpfull !!
